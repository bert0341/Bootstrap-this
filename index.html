Grid Systems

Using grid systems allows you to align text and items when a page reaches certain sizes. This means your text can enlarge and take up more of the page when it sizes to desktop and the opposite way

Text-1

("col-4 col-md-6 col-lg-8 text-center")

Text 1 will take-up 4 columns at the mobile version, 6 columns at tablet version, and 8 columns at the desktop version
